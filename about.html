About Me

From Mozambique to Portugal: A Platform Engineer's Journey

My Story

Born and raised in Maputo, Mozambique, my journey into technology began at the Portuguese School of Mozambique, where I discovered my passion for problem-solving and innovation. This passion led me to Portugal in 2016, where I pursued an Integrated Master's degree in Engineering and Management of Information Systems at the University of Minho.

During my academic journey, I immersed myself in the world of Big Data and distributed systems, working as a Research Grant holder at ISISE where I built and managed a Hadoop cluster for processing sensor data from road pavements. This experience sparked my fascination with infrastructure and large-scale data processing.

Currently, I'm part of the Programa Contacto at MC Digital (Sonae), where I work as a Platform Engineer, managing cloud infrastructure, automating deployments, and ensuring the reliability of critical systems that power one of Portugal's largest retail operations.

What I Do

As a Platform Engineer, I bridge the gap between development and operations, creating robust infrastructure that enables teams to deliver value faster and more reliably. My work involves:

  • Designing and implementing Infrastructure as Code using Terraform for Azure environments
  • Managing Linux systems and automating repetitive tasks with Ansible and Bash scripting
  • Configuring Big Data platforms including Hadoop, Databricks, and Cloudera
  • Providing technical support and solving complex infrastructure challenges
  • Ensuring security and compliance across all platform deployments

I believe in the power of automation to eliminate toil and enable engineers to focus on what truly matters: solving interesting problems and delivering value to users.
