About Me

From Mozambique to Portugal: A Platform Engineer's Journey

My Story

Born and raised in Maputo, Mozambique, my journey into technology began at the Portuguese School of Mozambique, where I discovered my passion for problem-solving and innovation. This passion led me to Portugal in 2016, where I pursued an Integrated Master's degree in Engineering and Management of Information Systems at the University of Minho.

During my academic years, I discovered a fascination with how systems work at scale and how infrastructure enables amazing things to happen. What started as curiosity about technology grew into a genuine love for building reliable systems that make people's lives easier.

Today, I work as a Platform Engineer at MC Digital (Sonae), where I get to combine my technical skills with my passion for solving real-world problems. Whether it's automating repetitive tasks or ensuring systems run smoothly, I find joy in the challenge of making complex things simple and reliable.

Food Bank Against Hunger

Social Action Volunteer | September 2024 | Porto, Portugal

Volunteering at the Food Bank Against Hunger in Porto has been an incredibly rewarding experience. As part of the social action team, I contribute to ensuring food security for vulnerable populations in the Porto region.

Activities & Responsibilities:

  • Receiving and sorting food donations from local businesses and individuals
  • Organizing food items by category and expiration date for efficient distribution
  • Distributing food packages to families in need with dignity and respect
  • Collaborating with team members to streamline the logistics of food distribution
  • Supporting community initiatives that raise awareness about food waste and hunger

This experience has taught me the importance of community solidarity and how small actions can make a significant difference in people's lives. It's a reminder that beyond technology and code, we all have a responsibility to contribute to our communities.

AMMA - Matosinhos Environment

Environmental Volunteer | November 2024 | Matosinhos, Portugal

Through Matosinhos City Council's environmental program AMMA (Association for the Environment of Matosinhos), I've been actively involved in protecting and preserving local ecosystems.

Conservation Activities:

  • Participated in invasive species removal projects to protect native biodiversity
  • Helped plant native tree species to restore natural habitats
  • Learned about local flora and fauna and the importance of ecosystem balance
  • Contributed to environmental education initiatives in the community
  • Worked alongside environmental experts and passionate volunteers

This hands-on environmental work has deepened my appreciation for nature and sustainability. It's shown me that protecting our environment requires active participation from everyone, and that even small contributions can help preserve our planet for future generations.
